MAM1000W is a wall-to-wall marathon. Don't wait until week 6 to start practicing — do problems from day one. The textbook's even-numbered questions are perfect self-test material.

Study Tip — MAM1000W

Do at least 20 practice problems per week. Past papers from 2019–2023 are available on BookBridge. Start revision 3 weeks before the exam.

ECO1010F seems straightforward until the midterm. The theory is easy but the application questions trip everyone up. Get a book with margin notes — they're worth gold.

Study Tip — ECO1010F

Spend 40% of your time on graphs and diagrams. The exam is 60% diagram interpretation. Past papers from 2020 onwards changed format — use those specifically.

"

For CSC1015F, the textbook matters less than consistent practice. But having a good reference copy with examples solved in the margins genuinely sped up my debugging.

Study Tip — CSC1015F

Write code every single day, even if it's just 20 lines. The practical test is harder than it looks. Use Python's built-in debugger — don't just print-statement your way to a solution.

"

LAW1010F requires you to memorise a lot of case names and ratios. I found it way easier with a tabbed copy of the textbook — I bought one from BookBridge already tabbed by the previous owner. Absolute lifesaver.

Study Tip — LAW1010F

Create a case law summary table: case name, parties, issue, holding, ratio. The exam will always include a problem-type question — practice applying cases to new facts.

I was sceptical about buying a second-hand book with someone else's notes in it. Turned out the previous owner's PSY1004F highlights aligned perfectly with what the lecturer tested. I got 72% using their framework.

Exam Prep — PSY1004F

Focus on the theoretical frameworks in chapters 3–7. The essay questions always ask you to apply a theory to a real-world scenario. Practice with 2022 and 2023 papers from BookBridge.

EEE3046W nearly broke me in second year. The textbook is massive. I bought a second-hand copy for R650 instead of R1,100 and used the money I saved for tutoring. Best financial decision of my degree.

Study Tip — EEE3046W

Master Fourier transforms before anything else. Everything in signals and systems builds on them. Budget 15 hours per week for this module — it's notorious for a reason.

ACC1006W is a grind but it's very learnable. The key is to never fall behind — each week's content builds on the last. I found that past papers from 2021+ are most representative of the current exam style.

Study Tip — ACC1006W

Do every practice question in the textbook. The exam questions are often directly adapted from them. Don't try to predict — prepare everything thoroughly.
